Why Now?

Today's social climate is characterized by a surge in psychological challenges like occupational pressure, social expectations, and bullying. Limited access to resources, stigma, and insufficient support worsen this crisis, affecting job performance and overall well-being.

Proactive measures are required to immediately address potential mental health issues, de-stigmatize mental health discussions, and enhance support systems across all types of institutions.
